Orzo Salad with Feta

Description

This refreshing Orzo Salad with Feta is perfect for summer picnics or as a light side dish. It's filled with fresh vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and a simple lemon vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orzo pasta
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook orzo pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked orzo, feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
