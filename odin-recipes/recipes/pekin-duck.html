Pekin Duck

Description

Pekin Duck is a traditional Chinese dish known for its crispy skin and succulent meat. It is often served with pancakes, hoisin sauce, and green onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole Pekin duck (about 5 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons five-spice powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • Green onions, thinly sliced
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Mandarin pancakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Rinse the duck and pat dry. Mix honey, soy sauce, and rice vinegar in a small bowl. Brush the mixture over the duck. Sprinkle with five-spice powder, salt, and white pepper.
  3. Place the duck on a rack in a roasting pan. Roast in the preheated oven for about 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until the skin is crisp and the meat is cooked through.
  4. Let the duck rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with green onions, hoisin sauce, and Mandarin pancakes.
